I'm a passionate Product Designer and UX practitioner based in Assam, India, pursuing my B.Des from Tezpur University. I combine design thinking, empathy, and creativity to build products that genuinely serve people.

My design philosophy is simple: every user deserves a product that feels made for them. Whether it's an inclusive biofeedback game or a neighbourhood skill-exchange app, I put people at the centre of every decision.
